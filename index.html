Arawak

Arawak is one of the first language that was spoken in Jamaican
irlands when people where trailed to the irlands of jamaica.
Interestingly the word Jamaica has been derived from the Arawak
word 'xaymaca' which means the 'land of wood and water' or the
'land of forest and water'. Arawak as a language of the Tainos
may not be in vogue but it has definitely contributed
to the enrichment of the English language.
